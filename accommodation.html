Toronto

The capital of the Canadian province of Ontario, Toronto is located within the Golden Horseshoe in Southern Ontario on the northern shore of Lake Ontario. Toronto has plenty of accommodation possibilities that you will be able to locate through for example: AirBnB and Booking.com, but the organising team also has made sure we have two cheaper arrangements for accommodation.

Accommodation recommendations and special arrangements can be seen below.

Holiday Inn Downtown Toronto: a group discount code is available for 10 standard rooms per night on a first come-first serve basis. Wireless internet is included in this rate.

Reservation: Guests can either call in to reserve at: 416-977-6655 OR 1-855- 914-9610

Or follow the below link to book online:
http://ichotelsgroup.com/redirect?path=rates&brandCode=HI&GPC=ZCZ&hotelCode=YYZCT&_PMID=99801505

Promo code: RES ID: ZCZ (2019 Intl Young Water Professionals Conf)

Dates: June 23-27, 2019

Cut-off date: May 24, 2019

Notes:

  • All individual reservations must be guaranteed by credit card.
  • Reservations may be cancelled only by calling the hotel directly and obtaining a cancellation number from a reservation agent. This will however affect the attrition fees that will be calculated at the end of the event.

Ryerson group accommodations: International Living/Learning Centre (ILC) & Pitman Hall (PIT)

Reservations: Guests can book their individual accommodation online: https://www.ryerson.ca/summer-stay/

Promo code: YWP2019

Dates: June 23-27, 2019

Cut-off date: April 23, 2019

Notes:

  • Guests must make their reservations directly online with the promo code provided.
  • If guests would like to stay for dates outside of your group booking, they must make their reservation online for the group dates and then contact reservations@ryerson.cato have their dates extended.
