All international visitors entering Canada are generally required to present a valid visa or passport issued by a Canadian Consular Official. Travel and Visa information for visitors to Canada can be found at the following websites:

  • www.cic.gc.ca/englisH/visit/index.asp
  • www.cic.gc.ca/englisH/visit/index.asp

If you require a Visa to enter Canada, we recommended that you apply for your visa at least 4 to 5 months prior to your departure date.
