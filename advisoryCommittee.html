Advisory Committee

Inter-generational learning

The role of the Advisory Committee is to provide advice and a mentorship role to the Programme Committee. These are highly acclaimed experts from within the water sector. The roles of the Advisory Committee it to:

  • Review the review of abstracts to ensure that the review of the abstracts is fair and transparent and that the abstracts which are selected to present are the top abstracts which were submitted.
