Empower Emerging Water leaders

We partner with some of the water sector’s leading companies and organizations and work together for a better water future.
As an innovative water industry, looking to showcase your technical, service and product solutions, and to engage with the best young water leaders, this is the investment platform for your brand.

GET IN TOUCH!

Shaping Our Water Future, Together.

We partner with some of the water sector’s leading companies and organizations and work together for a better water future.

Our sponsors and partners benefit from a unique opportunity to connect with young professionals from within and outside the water sector.

  1. Get personal access to over 300 young water professionals
  2. Get recognition within the global water and wastewater community as a company that supports and promotes emerging talent
  3. Gain extensive visibility by crossing many markets and broadening your international reach
  4. Gain valuable insights into the global Young Water Professionals market
  5. Gaining access to the Young Water Professionals fresh and innovative thinking
  6. Influencing Young Water Professionals as they progress in their career to decision making positions
  7. Provide professional development opportunities to your young staff by making them part of the Young Water Professionals segment
  8. Plant yourself firmly into the minds of key industry players
  9. Enjoy well-targeted networking opportunities

Gold Sponsers

Major advances in technology are creating extraordinary opportunities to solve water issues globally. Xylem is a leading global water technology company dedicated to solving the world’s most challenging water issues. We collaborate with our customers and partners to bring the right technology and solutions to the market to increase the productivity of, and optimize, water and wastewater operations. Our products and services move, treat, analyze, monitor and return water to the environment in public utility, industrial, residential and commercial building services settings. As a global leader in smart water, we are pioneering new ways to harness technology, data and innovation to deliver powerful and unprecedented insights to customers around the water cycle. We are excited about the incredible opportunities that new digital technologies are bringing to the water sector, and we are committed to driving innovation and advancing the conversation around digital transformation. We’re proud to sponsor the IWA International Young Water Professionals Conference 2019, and we look forward to participating in this important global dialogue about our water future. Let’s solve water. @Xyleminc

Bronze Sponsers

Trojan Technologies ensures greater water confidence and environmental stewardship for people, industries and municipalities, improving the lives of over one billion people globally. They enable customers to meet their water quality objectives by providing eco-efficient solutions that reduce and recover costs, energy, resources and space. Collaboratively solving problems with our customers, they deliver low-risk, innovative technologies that offer sustainable results. Trojan Technologies encompasses six businesses: Aquafine, Trojan Marinex, TrojanUV, Salsnes Filter, USP Technologies and VIQUA. The products and services provided by these businesses play vital roles in making various stages of the water treatment process more effective, efficient and sustainable.
